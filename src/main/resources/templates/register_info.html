Student Registration Process

How to Apply at iLearn High School

Step 1: Contact the School Administration

To begin the admission process, please contact the school administration. A staff member will verify your eligibility and begin pre-registration.

Step 2: Admin Pre-Registration

Once verified, the admin will create a pre-registration entry for you and send an email with a secure registration link.

Step 3: Complete Your Registration

Using the link in your email, complete the registration form online by setting your password and filling in your details.

Step 4: Final Confirmation

After submission, your registration will be reviewed and confirmed by our staff. You will then be able to log in and access your student portal.
